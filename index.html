My Story: From Entrepreneurship to Impact Strategy

My journey is not a straight line, but a rich tapestry woven from hands-on entrepreneurship, deep social research, and a relentless drive to understand the 'why' behind human behavior. This lived experience, combined with a Master's in Sociology and Global Change from the University of Edinburgh, informs my unique approach.
